Supplements
Creatine 5–10g/day — Increases sprint and explosive power by 5–15%. Start at 5g. Experienced athletes may use up to 10g with sufficient hydration. ⚠️ Under 18? Consult a parent or doctor first. Load for 4 weeks before pre-season. Most researched supplement in sport.
Caffeine 3–6mg/kg, 60 min before — Reduces perceived effort by 20–30%. Coffee works perfectly.
Magnesium 300mg before sleep — Over 60% of footballers are deficient. Reduces cramp risk and improves sleep quality.
Vitamin D3 2000–4000 IU/day — Essential for bone health and immune function.
Omega-3 2–3g EPA+DHA/day — Reduces inflammation and muscle soreness. Salmon 3x per week is ideal.
⚕️ Safety disclaimer
Nutrition advice in ProPath is educational only and does not replace medical or dietician advice. If you are under 18, have a health condition, or are unsure about any supplement, always consult a parent, doctor, or qualified professional first. Prioritise hydration — minimum 2.5L water daily for active athletes.
