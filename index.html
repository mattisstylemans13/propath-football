Supplement guide for footballers
Creatine monohydrate (3–5g/day)
Most researched supplement. Increases sprint and explosive power by 5–15%. Load for 4 weeks before pre-season.
Caffeine (3–6mg/kg, −60min)
Reduces perceived effort by 20–30%. Coffee works. Avoid if you don't use it in training.
Magnesium (300mg before sleep)
Over 60% of footballers are deficient. Reduces cramp risk and improves sleep quality.
Vitamin D3 (2000–4000 IU/day)
Essential for bone health and immunity. Most indoor players are deficient year-round.
Omega-3 (2–3g EPA+DHA/day)
Reduces inflammation and muscle soreness. Best from fatty fish or algae-based supplement.
